Here is a simple recipe to make a Bread Toast . It's easy to follow and will make your mouth water!

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1 egg
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions:

1.Heat a pan over medium heat.
2.Spread the butter on one side of each slice of bread.
3.Season the buttered side of the bread with salt and pepper.
4.Place the bread, buttered side down, in the pan.
5.Toast the bread for 2-3 minutes or until golden brown.
6.Flip the bread and toast the other side for another 2-3 minutes or until golden brown.
7.Serve hot and enjoy!
